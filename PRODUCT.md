# Product

## Register

product

## Users

One athlete (the owner) who runs and lifts. Sessions arrive automatically from
an Apple Watch (HealthFit → Drive sync) and from pasted Strong-app text; the
user checks in after a workout, on the couch, or when planning the week with
the built-in AI coach. Mobile and desktop both matter — post-workout checks are
usually on the phone.

## Product Purpose

A personal training log that combines running and strength data in one place:
upload/sync sessions, review each workout's numbers, track training load and
PRs, and get plans + feedback from an AI coach. Success = the athlete can see
at a glance whether they're improving and what to do next.

## Brand Personality

Focused athlete tool. Data-forward, calm, precise — like a race clock. The
interface stays quiet so the numbers carry the energy. Electric blue is
reserved for data and state, not decoration.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards: identical card grids, gradient stat tiles,
  corporate admin-panel chrome.
- Gamified fitness apps: badges, streak pressure, confetti, mascot energy.

## Design Principles

- **Numbers are the interface.** Mono, tabular, big where they matter; labels
  small and quiet. Every screen answers "am I improving?"
- **Blue means data.** The accent marks live values, trends, and state — never
  ornament. Primary actions are ink.
- **Earn every box.** Stats sit directly on the page; cards only when they
  group something real. No box-per-widget dashboards.
- **Motion shows mechanics.** Bars grow, meters fill, numbers count up —
  animation demonstrates the data arriving, never decorates. Reduced motion
  always respected.
- **Zero-friction capture.** Getting a workout in (sync, paste, drop a file)
  must never require typing what a device already knows.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA contrast for text; keyboard focus visible app-wide (single
  :focus-visible treatment).
- `prefers-reduced-motion` collapses movement to fades everywhere.
- No color-only meaning: trends pair color with direction glyphs/labels.
